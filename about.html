About LynchPin Advisory

Founded on the belief that strategic partnerships are the lynchpin of sustainable growth — connecting global expertise with African opportunity.

LynchPin Advisory is a premier strategic advisory firm dedicated to unlocking Africa's vast potential by connecting world-class expertise with local insight and opportunity.

We work at the intersection of sustainability, climate finance, green energy, and business strategy — partnering with governments, private sector leaders, NGOs, and international institutions to craft solutions that create lasting impact.

Our reach extends beyond Africa — into the Middle East, Europe, and the United States — creating a truly global bridge for capital, talent, and innovation to flow where it's needed most.

Meet the Founder

Hanisa Weru

18+ Years of Cross-Sector Experience

With over 18 years of experience in sustainability, business development, and strategic alliances, Hanisa specializes in crafting high-impact, scalable solutions tailored to Africa's unique challenges and opportunities. Her expertise lies in developing green energy initiatives, mobilizing climate finance, and integrating technology to accelerate sustainable growth across the continent.

She has collaborated with governments, NGOs, and private sector leaders to drive projects such as clean water access, green energy deployment in refugee camps, and women's financial inclusion programs — always with a focus on empowering communities and aligning local needs with global sustainability goals.
