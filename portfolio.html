Signvrse

Building a shared commercial language for a growing accessibility technology company.

Client

Signvrse

Sector

Accessibility Technology

Engagement

Sales Strategy & Sales Enablement

Signvrse had developed an innovative accessibility platform backed by a passionate team. As the business prepared for growth, its commercial execution needed to evolve alongside the product. LynchPin partnered with the leadership team to build a scalable commercial foundation by aligning messaging, strengthening sales capability, and creating structures that support sustainable growth.

Read Full Case Study

The Challenge

Signvrse had a strong product and a growing team, but no shared sales language, no formal training, and no clarity on where the structure needed to catch up with ambition. The product wasn't the problem—the commercial foundation was.

LynchPin's Approach

  • Positioning Framework: Built a shared language around What, Why, Who, Where, When that every pitch now runs on
  • Sales Clarity Workshop: Used the Johari Window to surface gaps between what the team knew and what the market assumed
  • Live Training: Elevator pitches, negotiation role-play, objection handling—practiced, not just discussed
  • Structural Diagnosis: Identified the sales bottleneck and proposed a Sales Support function to close it
  • Leadership Outputs: Recaps, briefs, and a clear next-steps roadmap after every session

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ One shared commercial narrative across the team
  • ✓ Practiced skills live, not just theorized
  • ✓ Leadership has a defined path—role clarity, KPIs, and comms—to build on
