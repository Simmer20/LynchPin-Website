Signvrse
July 2026 Sales Enablement
Sector: Accessibility Technology Engagement: Sales Strategy & Enablement

Building a shared commercial language for a growing accessibility technology company.

View Full Case Study

The Challenge

Signvrse had a strong product and a growing team, but no shared sales language, no formal training, and no clarity on where the structure needed to catch up with ambition. The product wasn't the problem—the commercial foundation was.

LynchPin's Approach

  • Positioning Framework: Built a shared language around What, Why, Who, Where, When
  • Sales Clarity Workshop: Used the Johari Window to surface messaging gaps
  • Live Training: Elevator pitches, negotiation, objection handling
  • Structural Diagnosis: Proposed a Sales Support function
  • Leadership Outputs: Recaps, briefs, and next-steps roadmap

Outcomes

  • ✓ One shared commercial narrative across the team
  • ✓ Practiced skills live, not just theorized
  • ✓ Leadership has a defined path—role clarity, KPIs, and comms
